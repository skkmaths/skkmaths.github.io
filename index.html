About me
Hi I am Sudarshan, I acquired my Ph.D from Tata Institute of Fundamental Research-Centre for Applicable Mathematics Bengaluru in April 2014. The core topic of my research is Partial differential equations, more precisely theory, numerics and computational methods of hyperbolic conservation laws.Presently I am a post doctoral fellow at TIFR CAM, formerly a post doctoral fellow at University of Concepcion, Chile, hosted by Prof. Raimund Bürger.
Research Interests
  • Nonlinear system of hyperbolic conservation laws.
  • Multicomponent polymer transport in EOR process.
  • Two phase flow problems in heterogeneous porous media.
  • Conservation laws with flux function allowed to be discontinuous in space variable.
  • High resolution numerical methods for conservation laws.
  • Discontinuous Galerkin methods for time dependent problems.
  • High order PDE like KDV equation.
