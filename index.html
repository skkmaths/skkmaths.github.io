Hi I am Sudarshan, I acquired my Ph. D from Tata Institute of Fundamental Research-Centre for Applicable Mathematics Bengaluru in April 2014. The core topic of my research is Partial differential equations, more precisely theory, numerical analysis and computational methods for hyperbolic conservation laws. Presently I am a post doctoral fellow at TIFR CAM Bengaluru, India.

Areas of current research:

  • Nonlinear system of hyperbolic conservation laws.
  • Multicomponent polymer transport in EOR process.
  • Two phase flow problems in heterogeneous porous media.
  • Conservation laws with flux function allowed to be discontinuous in space variable.
  • High resolution numerical methods for conservation laws.
  • Discontinuous Galerkin methods for time dependent problems.
